WHAT WE DO

Who We Are

We are a group of middle school students who are hoping to better our math skills. Our team consists of dozens of testsolvers and other members that work hard to create better problems.

Our Mission

Our mission, driven by our passion in mathematics, is to help other students improve in contest math and develop a passion in it. If you sound like someone who wants to help with that, you can join our team! JOIN US
