WHAT WE DO

Who We Are

We are a group of middle school students who are hoping to better our math skills. Our team consists of dozens of testsolvers and other members that work hard to create better problems.

Our Mission

Our mission, driven by our passion in mathematics, is to help other students improve in contest math and develop a passion in it. If you sound like someone who wants to help with that, you can contact us and join our team!

Contact Us

If you wish to contact us in order to join our team or have a question answered, you can do so by emailing team.mathimatika@gmail.com.
