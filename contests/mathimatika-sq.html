HOW IT WORKS

Before the contest

  1. To sign up, you will find a link on the homepage directing you to a Google Form which will enroll you in the competition. Please note that you can only sign up if you're a rising 9th grader or below (grade level referring to the grade you will be going into).
  2. Your submission time will be included in your problem solving time, so be sure to familiarize yourself with the Google Form.

During the contest

  1. You will have 45 minutes to submit and finish the 25 fill-in-the-blank problems.
  2. Tie-breaking: Two students who solves the same number of problems will be tie-broken by the following rule:
    The student who solved the last problem will be ranked higher. If two students both solved/both didn't solve it, the student who solved the second-to-last problem will be ranked higher, and so on.
  3. Each problem will be worth one point. You will not be penalized for any blank or wrong questions.
  4. We restate, you will find the problems in a link to a document on google drive on the top of our home page and as a reply in the AoPS announcement, so it would also be a good idea to bookmark that post.

After the contest

  1. Prizes announcement: prizes will be announced together with the results in a special and new AoPS announcement post. You will also find a new page on our website saying "results".
  2. Results policy: If you do not wish to see your username revealed on AoPS, you may email us privately at team.mathimatika@gmail.com, together with your name.
